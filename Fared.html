About me

Born in Malaysia but lived the majority of my life in Qatar.

Recently I've started on a streetwear fashion-brand online store, initially with a NodeJS stack which can be found at FlexKulture.com . While majority of the traffic is currently being relied through the invesments of online advertisments, my goal is to progress it until it can be recognized as a private label fashion brand.

I started graphic designing freelancing at the age of 13 and eventually taught myself programming in Python, Java, Javascript and C. I began developing mobile applications at the age of 14 and published a few games on the Appstore and Google Playstore. I programmed a timetable app on the Appstore for my school when I was 15. Utilizing C# in Unity 3D, I began publishing 3D arcade games, some which have garnered over 500,000 installs. I then started to teach myself some in depth website development in the back-end as well as the front-end, mainly with the use of the NodeJS stack.

My hobbies include a variety of sports such as basketball, football, squash, badminton, tennis and volleyball, which I try to involve myself in on a weekly basis. I also play games such as League of Legends of which I'm in one of the official UoM teams in their esports society.
